About

Hello, world. My name is Yura Kim, I was born in New York City then moved to Delaware at age 8. There I attended a magnet middle and high school for the arts then moved on to attend the Delaware College of Art and Design. Throughout my year at DCAD, I felt unfulfilled. so I made a life altering decision to change my major to software engineering and transfer to the Rochester Institute of Technology.
